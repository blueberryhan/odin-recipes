Bowl of Cereal

Description

The most iconic dish invented by world class renowned chef, Jimmy Neutron.
It's really tasty and has milk in it.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cereal

Steps

  1. Go to Fred Meyer
  2. Ask worker at the Fred Meyer jeweller where the cereal is.
  3. Stare at their lips with your eyes wide
  4. Go to the cereal aisle
  5. Purchase the cereal of your choice
  6. Go home
  7. Milk your cow in the backyard
  8. Set up 1:15 ratio titration series to purify milk
  9. Add the reagent and perform alpha-sigma procedural algorithm
  10. Pour milk into bowl
  11. Eat it
